How To Play

This is the game area - Zombies will pop up from behind the headstones and your cursor turns to crosshairs to shoot the Zombies! Beware, there is a loud gun shot sound every time you manage to click on a zombie, so use caution with your volume!

This is the area where you will see how much time you have remaining and also how many points you have scored.

Click this button to start the game, there is an alert message to tell you about the loud gun shot sound that features in the game, there is a 2 second delay to allow you to return to the game area and then get ready to shoot some Zombies!

Click this button to change the difficulty level. This will change the amount of time you have to get as many points as possible.

This button will toggle from the colours you see when the page loads, to a dark mode.

By clicking this button, you can view the leaderboard. You also have the option to save your name and score at the end of a game.
